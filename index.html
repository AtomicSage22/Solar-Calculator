Solar Panel Calculator

Once you have determined the system size, you can divide it by the rated output of the solar panels to determine the number of panels needed. Keep in mind that the rated output of the solar panels may vary depending on their efficiency and other factors. This solar panel calculator is the tool to calculate the number of solar panels required as per the energy needed by an average household in unit watt. It also shows the area covered by the solar panels and price according to the current market rate.
